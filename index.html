About Me

Hello! I am Sakshi Ghosalkar. I'm a UX/UI designer, design thinker, experience strategist, generative artist, and ambivert who loves people. I approach design and leadership with a focus on people, developing empathy for both the people who manufacture and the people who utilise the things I help bring to life. My professional practise blends design thinking, user research, and experience strategy, all while maintaining a laser-like focus on the customer. I've been investigating nfts in my spare time.
